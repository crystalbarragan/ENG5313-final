Our story

We're former teachers, Texas State grads and writing professionals who geek out over movies, tech and publishing great user manuals.

Small beginnings. Big impact.

Userdocs.io began as a group of students interested in offering technical writing services to startups in the San Marcos and Austin, TX area. The team quickly established a reputation for incredible work, and grew into a small company working from within the Texas State University campus.

Today, Userdocs.io works with over 70 companies and 200 developers to create beautiful documentation the affordable way.
